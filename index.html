About Jai2k

Hi, my name's Aidan, or Jai2k. I’m a 24-year-old producer, composer, and sound designer based out of Miami. Music is a big part of who I am: I grew up with, and was raised by talented musicians on both sides of my family. At 12, my dad introduced me to my first production software, Logic Pro 9. From there I was hooked. By the time I was 15, I began releasing electronic music under the alias Intrex, drawing inspiration from artists like Noisia, Koan Sound, and Skrillex.

Over the years, I’ve picked up experience across genres like house, techno, hip hop, pop, drum n bass, bass music, trap, experimental, ambient, cinematic, and more. In 2022, I retired the Intrex alias to develop Jai2k, a project focused more on raw artistic expression through deeper sonic experimenation and exploration.

I now offer professional sound services for ads, sonic branding, short films, YouTube videos, and video games — specializing in immersive and dynamic sound design & music. As I'm preparing my debut album in 2025, I'm excited about the role of artistic sound design in music and multimedia, and the increasing creative freedom we enjoy from rapidly evolving audio tech & collective collaboration. Whether you’re a filmmaker, brand, indie game developer, or artist, I’m eager to hear about your project and how I could help bring it to life!
